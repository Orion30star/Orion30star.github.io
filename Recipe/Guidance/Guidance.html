Cookie

Guidance on how to make this cookie

Step one

Add the Butter in a bowl

Step two

Add the sugar to the Butter

  • Start mixing the butter and the sugar together
Step three

After the sugar and butter have been mixed together and its in multiple "crumble"

  • add the eggs.
Step four

While the dough is mixing, prepare the flour and add baking soda to the flour.

Step five

Now that the our eggs and crumbled dough have mixed together, we are gonna add the flour/baking Soda and Vanilla extract together

This is how it should look like

After all the steps, this is how it should look like! If your dough looks like this, then well done, we have completed the hardest part of this journey and we are on the right path

Step six

Now that we have our dough, we have to take dark chocolate, and slice it small pieces, so we get chocolate "chips"!

Step seven

Now add the "chocolate chips" to the dough.

Step eight

Now take a spoon full of dough in your hands, make it flat and ad Nutella in the center point. And make it to a circle

Step nine

This is how it should look like.

Step ten

Put the tray(where you have layed the cookies on) in the Oven, and let it stay there for 14 minutes for 180 degrees in hot air plus!

Step eleven

After its done with the oven, take it out and enjoy lads!
